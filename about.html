About GreenVision

Discover our journey, values, and the passionate team behind your perfect vision

Our Story

Visionary Beginnings

Founded in 2005, GreenVision began as a small optical shop in the heart of Srinagar with a simple mission: to provide high-quality eyewear that combines style, comfort, and affordability. Our founder, Dr. Arjun Sharma, an optometrist with over 20 years of experience, noticed a gap in the market for eyewear that didn't compromise on either fashion or function.

What started as a single store has now grown into a trusted brand with multiple locations across Jammu and Kashmir, serving thousands of satisfied customers each year. We've maintained our commitment to personalized service while expanding our product range to include the latest trends in eyewear fashion.

At GreenVision, we believe that everyone deserves to see clearly and look great doing it. Our team of skilled optometrists and fashion consultants work together to ensure you find the perfect pair of glasses that not only corrects your vision but also complements your unique style.

Our Values

Vision Care First

Your eye health is our top priority. We use only the highest quality materials and latest technology to ensure optimal vision correction and protection.

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices, from using sustainable materials in our frames to implementing recycling programs for old glasses.

Community Focus

We believe in giving back to our community through vision care programs, free eye check-up camps, and supporting local initiatives.

Quality Craftsmanship

Each pair of glasses is crafted with precision and attention to detail, ensuring durability, comfort, and timeless style.

Our Journey

2005

GreenVision was founded by Dr. Arjun Sharma with a single store in Srinagar, focusing on quality eyewear and personalized service.

2010

Expanded to three locations across Jammu and Kashmir, introducing our first line of eco-friendly frames made from sustainable materials.

2015

Launched our online store, making GreenVision eyewear accessible to customers across India with nationwide shipping.

2018

Introduced advanced lens technology including blue light filtering and progressive lenses for digital eye strain protection.

2020

Despite challenges, we launched our virtual try-on feature, allowing customers to preview frames from the comfort of their homes.

2023

Celebrated 18 years of service with over 50,000 satisfied customers and recognition as Jammu & Kashmir's leading eyewear provider.
