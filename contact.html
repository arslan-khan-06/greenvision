Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for delivery within 2-3 business days. International shipping times may vary.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items in their original packaging. Prescription glasses can be returned if there's an error in the prescription we provided.

Do you offer prescription lenses?

Yes, we offer prescription lenses for all our frames. You can provide your prescription during checkout or visit one of our stores for an eye test.

How do I know my frame size?

Each product page includes detailed measurements. You can also use our virtual try-on feature or visit our size guide for assistance in finding the perfect fit.

Do you have physical stores?

Yes, we have multiple stores across the country. Visit our store locator to find the nearest location to you.
